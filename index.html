Today's special: Miyabi Sushi

Sushi is a staple food of the Asian influenced, Pacific Northwest. There are many options in today's age but nothing beats the quality of Miyabi Sushi.

Table of Contents

  • Summary
  • Location
  • Menu Features
  • Atmosphere
  • Verdict

Summary

Miyabi Sushi is based in Tukwila, WA. It is founded by Masa-san and Hisako who are both native Japanese folk. According to the official website, Masa-san has been an executive chef in Tokyo and Seattle since 1990. That's how you know his is all legitimate, authentic Nippon cuisine.

Location

16820 Southcenter Parkway, Tukwila, Washington 98188

Menu Features

I've been going to Miyabi Sushi for many years with my family, especially on special occassions. Here is a small list of my favorite sushi rolls on their menu:

  • Miyabi Roll
  • Rainbow Roll
  • Spider Roll
  • Dancing Albacore
  • Sex On The Beach
  • Orange Delight
  • Tropical Roll
  • Red And White
  • Caterpillar Roll

I also highly reccommend their shrimp tempura as an appetizer as well.

Atmosphere

The interior of Miyabi Sushi is sleek with subtle Japanese design accents in the wall frames and tables. It's very conservative and subdued in comparison to your typical local Asian diner. You'll find no chopstick fonts and minimal usage of red in here.

Verdict

Well, my verdict is that this is good stuff and if you're looking to try some sushi, Miyabi Sushi is a solid place to start.
